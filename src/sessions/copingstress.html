COPING WITH STRESS

DESCRIPTION


Coping With Stress is a buzzword that you may have heard recently? If you do a quick search on Pinterest, you will find hundreds of pins about coping skills… all from Coping Skills Bingo to create your Wheel of Coping Skills. Blogs and articles like these can provide useful tips on how to use problem-solving skills, but they don't always provide some very important background information… things like coping skills, and why you use coping skills? So, this article is my own personal attempt to answer similar questions!
