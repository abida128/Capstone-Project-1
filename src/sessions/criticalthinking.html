CRITICAL THINKING

DESCRIPTION


Critical Thinking is when the information is collected in the form of any experience or view and is used to analyze, use or evaluate the information collected it is called critical thinking. There are four main aspects to analytical thinking; self-discipline, self-control, and self-discipline.

There are 7 steps that help to improve the process of deep thinking.

  • Identify the problem
  • Gather the details
  • Check the details
  • Make a decision based on the accuracy of the information
  • Measure
  • Perform the analysis
  • Describe your analysis.
