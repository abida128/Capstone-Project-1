DECISION MAKING

DESCRIPTION


Decision Making is the process in which one has to make a rational and explainable choice out of more than one option. The term decision making is also referred to as a cognitive process. There are three types of decision making Strategic decision where upper-level management of the organization makes long-term decisions that set the direction for an entire organization. decision where decisions regarding how things will be done are taken. Operational decision where day-to-day decisions are made by the lower-level employees to run the operations of the organization smoothly.
